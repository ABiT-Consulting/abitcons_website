KSA ZATCA-COMPLIANT

ZATCA E-Invoicing Integration (KSA)

A ZATCA Phase-1 & Phase-2 compliant e-invoicing solution with real-time clearance, reporting, and seamless ERP integration.

  • ZATCA Phase-1 & Phase-2 compliance
  • UBL 2.1 XML invoice generation
  • Real-time clearance and reporting
Full details & features

ABiT's KSA E-Invoicing solution ensures full compliance with Saudi Arabia's ZATCA (FATOORA) regulations. The system supports XML invoice generation, cryptographic signing, QR code creation, real-time clearance, and reporting. It integrates seamlessly with SAP Business One and other ERP systems, enabling automated compliance while maintaining security, scalability, and audit readiness.

  • ZATCA Phase-1 & Phase-2 compliance
  • UBL 2.1 XML invoice generation
  • QR code, UUID, and hash chaining
  • Digital signing and certificate management
  • Real-time clearance and reporting
  • SAP Business One integration (HANA & SQL)
  • API integration for custom systems
  • Error handling and re-submission workflow
  • Secure archiving and audit trail
  • Multi-branch and high-volume support
